Road Freight Introduction

When goods have to be delivered to your customer’s door, road freight is still indispensable. And will remain so in future. In recent years, El Tesla World Shipping Shipment has expanded the options for European distribution by road freight by adding dozens of lanes. We make up our own daily schedules for packed product lines. This applies both to full and part truck loads.

Transport Solutions

El Tesla World Shipping Shipment is the number one logistics supply chain director with a maritime character. In addition to overseas import, export and warehousing activities, we also handle the distribution of your goods to and from any destination in the World. For more than 20 years, our business locations situated at inland road, rail and water terminals have been the foundation of our operations. Our logistics services would not be complete without a rapid and efficient transport service that is perfectly in line with these terminals and which allow us to deliver goods right to your customer’s door.

Quality and Reliability

Your business needs the right goods in the right place at the right time. In other words: delivery reliability. That’s why our efforts are registered in KPIs. Thanks to our modern tracking & tracing systems, we measure and evaluate these indicators per shipment. Our modern information techniques allow us to analyze your shipment precisely. Our logistics engineers use this information to:

  • check the order intake processing.
  • measure and evaluate KPIs continually.
  • optimise your logistics process proactively.
